Subfolder for problems from the Codewars platform. Problems are community-generated and solutions are checked via fixed and randomised tests upon submission. Most solutions included here will be the author's, though if better solutions have been submitted by the community on Codewars (in terms of optimisation, syntax, and logic) this will be mentioned. Descriptions taken from the relevant problem's page has been copied in to provide context on what the code is meant to achieve.
